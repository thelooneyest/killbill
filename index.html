Kill Bill Volume 1

A Film by Quentin Tarantino

Synopsis

The Bride wakens from a four-year coma. Before she was 'The Bride', she was part of the Deadly Viper Assissination Squad, led by Bill. When she tried to leave the squad, they tried to assassinate her. Now she's back and out for revenge. She will reap a swatch of bloody revenge until she reaches her final target where she will . . . KILL BILL.
