Kill Bill


Volume 1

A Film by Quentin Tarantino

Synopsis

Uma Thurman stars as "The Bride", a deadly assassin who is a member of the Deadly Viper Assassination Squad. When she tries to quit the assassin business, her former boss Bill decides to take her out. Even after taking a bullet to the head, she doesn't die ...instead falling into a coma.

Four years later, 'The Bride' wakens from her coma with revenge on her mind. Now she'll have to fight her way through a deadly array of enemies to get revenge. She will reap a swath of bloody revenge until she reaches her final target where she will . . . KILL BILL.
